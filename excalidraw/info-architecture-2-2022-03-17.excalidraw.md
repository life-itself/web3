Where do ideologies fit in?

- they help us understand meaning of normative terms like 
fairness.
- they provide a contextual worldview that clarifies the 
debate

Also confusingly (and we should avoid this) ideologies can 
equate to "big claims" (or even aspirations).

=> (suggest) reducing or eliminating the term ideology. (if we
do have to use it with its specific meaning of "worldview" 
let's just use "worldview" which is more neutral)

Ideologies / imaginaries will show up again in the part of 
the project around sharing and addressing the "so many 
people are into this" type of question or even "why does X 
group believe Y"? (the "voting" aspect of sensemaking)
